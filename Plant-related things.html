nature

The wildlife of Sri Lanka includes its flora and fauna and their natural habitats. Sri Lanka has one of the highest rates of biological endemism 16% of the fauna and 23% of flowering plants are endemic The mountains and the southwestern part of the country, known as the "wet zone", receive ample rainfall . Most of the southeast, east, and northern parts of the country comprise the "dry zone", which receives between 1200 and 1900 mm of rain annually.
